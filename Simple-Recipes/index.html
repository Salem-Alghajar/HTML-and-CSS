Simple Recipes

Featured Recipe: Pizza & Broccoli

Instructions: Pre-heat oven to 425°F. Roll out pizza dough and top with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and your favorite toppings. Roast broccoli florets with olive oil, salt, and parmesan. Bake both until golden brown and delicious.

Easy Garlic Pasta

Instructions: Boil pasta until al dente. In a pan, sauté minced garlic in olive oil until fragrant. Toss the cooked pasta in the garlic oil, add red pepper flakes, fresh parsley, and a generous sprinkle of parmesan cheese.

Lemon Herb Chicken

Instructions: Marinate chicken breasts in lemon juice, olive oil, rosemary, and thyme. Sear in a hot skillet for 6-7 minutes on each side until cooked through. Serve with a side of roasted vegetables.

Glazed Pork Chops

Instructions: Season pork chops with salt and pepper. Sear in a pan. Create a simple glaze using honey, soy sauce, and a dash of apple cider vinegar. Pour over the chops and simmer until sticky and fully cooked.

Buttermilk Biscuits

Instructions: Mix flour, baking powder, and salt. Cut in cold butter until crumbly. Stir in buttermilk just until combined. Pat out the dough, cut into rounds, and bake at 400°F until golden brown and flaky.

Baked Salmon

Instructions: Place salmon fillets on a baking sheet. Brush with a mix of melted butter, garlic, and dill. Bake at 375°F for 12-15 minutes until the fish flakes easily with a fork.

Simple Berry Parfait

Instructions: In a glass, layer Greek yogurt, your favorite granola, and a mix of fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries). Repeat the layers and top with a drizzle of honey.

Our Story

Simple Recipes was born out of a single kitchen in the heart of the U.S. with a clear vision: to strip away the intimidation of gourmet cooking. We believe that a great meal shouldn't require a culinary degree or a pantry full of obscure ingredients. Our philosophy centers on the beauty of fresh produce, high-quality proteins, and the joy of sharing a table with loved ones.

From the first-time cook to the busy parent, we provide a curated collection of tried-and-true favorites that prioritize flavor without the fuss. Every technique is vetted by our team to ensure your time in the kitchen is both productive and peaceful. Welcome to our community—where every dish is a celebration of simplicity.
